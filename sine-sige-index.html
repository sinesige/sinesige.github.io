KISAPMATA @ 4pm

Dir. by Mike de Leon

A MOVING LIFE CHANGES IN A BLINK.

A retired police sergeant has an unnatural strangehold over his wife and daughter. His claustrophobically enclosed world is threatened when his daughter Mila finds herself pregenant and was forced to marry Noel. He attempts to extend his influence over his son-in-law, who resists; there is a confrontation.

cw: abuse, incest, familicide, sexual abuse, psychological abuse

PAN DE SALAWAL @ 7pm

Dir. by Che Espiritu

BELIVE IN MIRACLES.

A special little homeless girl travels about the Philippines curing the afflicted with her mysterious healing powers, until one day she befriends a sick old man who for some perplexing reason she is unable to cure. The unlikely pair are drawn to each other and their lives are forever changed by the unique frienship and magical journey together.

cw: depression
