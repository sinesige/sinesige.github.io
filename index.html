Sine! Sige! (#001) is proud to feature the internationally proclaimed "queen of sensual cinema", filmmaker Isabel Sandoval's breakout third film Lingua Franca.

The film follows Olivia, an undocumented migrant worker living as a caretaker as she attempts to figure out how to attain green card status. As her initial opportunities of a marriage of convenience falls through, she becomes involved with her employer’s grandson- a slaughterhouse worker recently returning from rehab. Lingua Franca is a character study- an intimate portrait of transwomanhood, a rendition of post-2016 election america underneath the trump regime and ICE’s violent removals of undocumented citizens, and the socioeconomic politics of care and it’s many forms and transactions.

cw: strong language, nudity, sexual scenes, transphobia.
